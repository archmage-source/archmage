Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 February 2026

ARCHMAGE Studio (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the UK Data Protection Act 2018 and the UK GDPR.

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us through our website form, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Your message

2. How We Collect Your Information

Your information is collected directly when you fill out and submit the contact form on our website. We use Formspree as the service provider to securely process and deliver your messages to us.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your enquiries or messages
  • Provide the services you request
  • Maintain communication with you regarding our projects or updates (if agreed)

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Our legal basis for processing your personal data is your consent when you submit the contact form.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Formspree, as our form processor, temporarily stores your message to deliver it securely to us.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry. After this period, we securely delete your data.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights under the UK GDPR:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the contact form or email us directly.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies or embed third-party content that sets cookies. All YouTube videos on our site are linked via thumbnails to avoid cookie banners.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “last updated” date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us via our contact form.
